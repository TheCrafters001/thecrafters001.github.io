Rules

Note: These rules also go along with the GameJolt Site Guidelines. Please follow those rules as well.

(Looking for ban appeals? Go here)

Ban Evasion isn’t allowed

If we banned you in the past and you try to get around the ban using an Alternative account, you will get banned again. The reason for your ban is listed as “Ban Evasion”. You will have to go to your primary account to see why.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, you will be banned forever. Your alt account will never get unbanned, even if your main one does.

Discrimination isn’t allowed

Discrimination about a persons' race, ethnicity, sexuality, etc. isn’t allowed. These behaviors will get you banned from the community forever.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, you will be banned forever.

Do NOT wish harm against others

Wishing harm against others is NOT allowed. Wishing harm against others will get you banned as we try to keep the community as safe as possible.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, you will be banned forever.

Don’t Backtalk the moderators

We do not tolerate backtalking to the moderators.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, you will be banned forever.

No Adult Content

Adult Content isn’t allowed on the community. This is to keep young users from seeing things that they should not see.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, you will be banned forever.

No Harassing Others

We try to keep the community as safe as possible. To do this, harassing others isn’t allowed.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, your first offense will get you banned for a week. Your second offense will get you banned forever.

Please do not share any personal information in any channel

We respect the privacy of others. If you start sharing any personal information about a person or group, you will get banned from the community.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, your first offense will get you banned for a year. Your second offense will get you banned forever.

Discussing other games is okay, as long as it is in the Random Channel

This community is primarily based off of Team Fortress 2, a Multiplayer First Person Shooter developed by Valve Software. While we do not want people to post about random games in other channels, it is fine if you post about the game in the random channel. You will not get banned, posts will be moved. However, if offense is repeated, you may get a very short 1 hour ban to clear out the channels.

This is not a bannable offense. Your posts will get moved to random, or get ejected. If you get banned for this, the ban will only last one hour. We only ban you for this long to clear out any posts that are unrelated to the community.

If you would like to re-enact any Team Fortress 2 Meme or Promotional video, please do so in the Memes channel.

This is to keep everything nice and organized. We wouldn’t want to have “Heavy is Dead” in the Gameplay Channel, now would we? However, if offense is repeated, you may get a very short 1 hour ban to clear out the channels.

This is not a bannable offense. Your posts will get moved to Memes, or get ejected. If you get banned for this, the ban will only last one hour. We only ban you for this long to clear out any posts that are unrelated to the community.

No Spamming

Spamming the community is where you create a bunch of posts in quick succession. Doing this is not allowed, as we want users to see other posts in the community.

This is a bannable offense. If you break this rule, your first offense will get you banned for a week. Your second offense will get you banned for a month. Your third offense gets you banned forever. If you get banned for this, the ban will only last one hour. We only ban you for this long to clear out any posts that are unrelated to the community.
