Welcome to the Shorebarrow Region!

Here, we have a variety of Pokémon from other regions! From Kanto to Galar! We have out own Pokémon League on this tiny island too, and we hope you have fun while taking part in it. We have Gym Leaders that specialize in different types of Pokémon, from Rock- to Fighting-, all the way to Dark- and Fairy-type Gym Leaders! And they aren't messing around! Do you have the skill to take them on?!

Team Titanium

There is a team lurking about! Team Titanium! It is said that they are looking for some sort of energy that the legendary Pokémon Rayquaza gives off... They are destroying Pokémon Habitats to find it! And they are getting closer by the day!

Delta Type Pokémon

In this region, there is a special energy called Delta Energy. If a Pokémon has had prolonged contact with Delta Energy over many generations, it will have a different appearance, and its types will change! Some say it even gives them an energy boost when they come in contact with a special stone.
