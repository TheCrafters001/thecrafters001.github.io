Team Titanium

Be warned! There is an orginization floating around the region, and they aren't playing nice! They are looking for some sort of energy called Delta Energy, and to find it, they are destroying Pokémon's habitat to find it! Each day that passes, they get closer and closer to finding the source! Can you put a stop to their plans?

Delta Species & Typing!

Delta Energy is a type of energy that allows a Pokémon to change forms when exposed to it over a very long period of time. Its appearance will change, and so will its typing. Scientist have no idea what causes such a change.
